SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS
Samagra Gune | 25BCE10462
=============================================

DO'S
-----
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your projects, skills, and achievements.
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a professor or employer seeing it.
3. Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal content.
4. Engage respectfully in online discussions, even when you disagree with someone.
5. Share your academic work, certifications, and learning progress on professional platforms.

DON'TS
-------
1. Do not share personal information like your phone number, address, or college ID publicly.
2. Do not post or repost content without verifying whether it is true or accurate.
3. Do not use social media to vent frustration about classmates, professors, or your institution.
4. Do not engage with or respond to online arguments, trolls, or hate speech.
5. Do not neglect your digital footprint — old posts can and do affect future opportunities.

=============================================
Remember: What goes online stays online.
Be Smart, Be Safe, Be Professional.
